Our Services

iConsult provides a diverse range of advisory services across eight key areas to clients in Pakistan. These services cater to various sectors with a specialized focus tailored to each client's needs.

Business Risk Services

At iConsult, we offer Business Risk Services (BRS) designed to enhance corporate governance and transparency. Our approach involves strengthening internal controls, optimizing the use of information technology, and making operational improvements. Services under BRS include governance, risk and compliance, operational improvement, strategic services, assurance services, third-party validation, monitoring, evaluation, and DLI Verification.

Transaction Advisory

Our Transaction Advisory services are crucial, encompassing Corporate Finance and Public Sector Finance. We guide clients through capital raising, acquisitions, disposals, and securing broader market presence. This includes handling business sales, capital markets interactions, private equity, resource mobilization, mergers & acquisitions, and management buy-outs. For the public sector, we specialize in project and infrastructure finance, public-private partnerships, development financing, sovereign financing, and resource mobilization.

Climate Change and Green Finance

Recognizing the critical challenges and opportunities presented by climate change, iConsult engages in Climate Change and Green Finance. We support our clients in developing effective climate strategies, structuring climate-aligned investments, and integrating climate considerations into their financial services and policies. Our offerings include climate investment strategies, blended finance for climate initiatives, climate risk and financial disclosure, and climate investment due diligence.

Organization Development

iConsult assists organizations facing operational challenges by offering services in restructuring and re-organization. We focus on diagnosing problems, integrating resources, and identifying a range of solutions tailored to each organization’s needs to foster a culture of sustainable improvement.

Strategy and Growth

Our Strategy and Growth services help clients manage and navigate their expansion through careful market research and strategic planning. We aid in setting goals, challenging assumptions, resource deployment, and strategic priority identification to ensure successful strategy implementation and system development.

Human Resource Management

Human Resource Management is another area where iConsult excels, providing HR placement services, systems design, and operational outsourcing. Our solutions are designed to address the most pressing HR issues in economies like Pakistan’s, enhancing organizational function and employee management.

Social Policy Sector

In the Social Policy Sector, iConsult leverages extensive experience to advise on health, education, poverty alleviation, and cultural initiatives. We conduct surveys, design infrastructure development plans, and oversee funding and implementation, ensuring effective results in social policy engagements.

Technology Services

Our Technology Services Division supports all areas of operation with IT strategy preparation, deal support, planning, implementation, and product development, both internally and for clients. This division ensures that technological advancements support and enhance the overall service delivery of our clients.
